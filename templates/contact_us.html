Frequently Asked Questions
How do I track my order?

To track your delivery, you can follow these steps:
Step 1: Log in to your account.
Step 2: Click on the "My Account" button and select "Orders" from the dropdown menu.
Step 3: Locate the order for which you want to track the delivery and click on the "View Details" button.
Step 4: On the order details page, look for the delivery status under the "Order Information" section. If the delivery status is "Shipped," you can click on the "Track Package" button.
Step 5: This will take you to the delivery tracking page, where you can view the current status of your delivery and any available tracking information.
Step 6: If you have any concerns about the delivery status, you can contact our customer service team by calling our number +2348143556118

How can I cancel my order?

To cancel an order:
Step 1: Log in to your account.
Step 2: Go to your "Orders" page.
Step 3: Find the order you want to cancel.
Step 4: Click on "See Details" for that order.
Step 5: Click the "Cancel Order" button and follow the steps to confirm the cancellation.

How can I return my order?

If you're not satisfied with your purchase, Tehilians makes it easy for you to return your items and receive a refund. To create a return, simply follow these easy steps.
All items must be in their original condition and packaging to be eligible for a return. Don't forget to keep your invoice with you.
Initiate your return.
Step 1: Log in to your account and go to "Orders".
Step 2: Click on the order of the item(s) you want to return.
Step 3: Select the item(s) you want to return and provide a reason for the return. Give more details to help us understand the issue.
Step 4: Choose your preferred refund method.
Step 5: Select whether you would like to personally drop off the returned item or have Us pick it up.
Step 6: Review your information and submit your return request.
